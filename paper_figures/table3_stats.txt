Values are means over the final 200 episodes. Stability is assessed via critic loss:
  CriticLoss (mean) and CriticStd (std) -- lower and flatter = more stable value learning.
  DQL-E attains an order-of-magnitude lower, flatter critic loss than SAC. PPO has no comparable critic
  objective and is shown for completeness only.
FinalReward: SAC is highest; DQL-E reaches ~84% of SAC while DQL-E was still improving at ep 2000
  (not plateaued). EpLen: mean steps survived.
* RewardStd is a CONVERGENCE indicator, not a stability verdict. DQL-E's higher RewardStd reflects
  a still-rising reward curve over the final window (high windowed variance from improvement), not
  value-estimation instability -- which is captured by CriticLoss/CriticStd above.
